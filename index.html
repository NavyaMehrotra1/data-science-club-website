Data Science Club @ Johns Hopkins University

What is the Data Science Club

The Data Science Club is JHU's premier student-led project based data science initiative to teach, explore and build with data.

What do we do?

Our format: kickoff workshops and weekly data labs.

  • Kickoff workshops: teaching the basics of data science in particular field, like important data sets and tools
  • Weekly data labs: Dedicated time to work on themed projects!
